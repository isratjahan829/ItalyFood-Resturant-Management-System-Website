Pizza

An Italian dish consisting of a flattened round base dough baked with a sprinkling of tomato sauce and cheese, usually with the addition of meat or vegetables.

Bahan

  • 200 g ready-made bolognese sauce
  • 150 g mozzarella cheese, coarsely grated
  • 100 g tomat merah, potong melintang 2 bagian, iris tebal 0,5 cm
  • 100 g jamur kancing segar, iris tebal 0,5 cm, tumis hingga layu
  • 450 g tepung terigu serba guna
  • 1 sdt (4 g) garam
  • 300 ml air
  • 1½ sdt (6 g) ragi instan
  • 1½ sdm minyak zaitun
  • 3 sdm minyak zaitun
  • 100 g bawang bombai, cincang halus
  • 4 siung bawang putih, cincang halus
  • 50 g paprika merah, potong dadu kecil
  • 300 g daging cincang
  • 1 sdt oregano kering
  • ½ sdt basil kering
  • ¾ sdt garam
  • ¾ sdt merica hitam, gerus kasar



Cara Membuat

  • Pizza bread: Put all the ingredients in the mixing bowl, knead until smooth. Cover the dough with plastic wrap or a clean napkin, let it rise twice (90-120 minutes).
  • Flatten the dough, divide it into 3 equal parts. Wrap each dough in plastic wrap, store in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. Set aside.
  • Topping: Heat olive oil in a skillet, saute onion and garlic until fragrant. Add red pepper and green pepper, cook until fragrant.
  • Add minced meat, oregano, basil, salt, and coarse pepper, cook stirring until meat is cooked. Lift.
  • Preparation: Take 1 piece of pizza dough, roll it with a rolling pin slowly until it is round in shape with a diameter of 20 cm or press the pizza dough using your fingertips until it forms the desired size. Place the dough on a flat baking sheet with baking paper base.
  • Grease the surface of the dough with 2-3 tbsp tomato sauce, set aside 1 cm of the edges of the dough so as not to teroles sauce. Add 2-3 tbsp stir-fried meat on top. Sprinkle with part mozzarella cheese, arrange tomato pieces and stir-fry mushrooms on top of cheese.
  • Bake the pizza in a hot oven at 200°C until cooked through and the edges of the dough are browned (10-15 minutes). Take it out, cut it into pieces to taste. Serve immediately.
